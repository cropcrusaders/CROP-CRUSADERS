Weather Tools

  • The Weather Channel ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.7)

    Best for daily forecasts and long-range weather predictions, with detailed radar maps and alerts.

  • AccuWeather ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5.0)

    Known for hyper-localized and minute-by-minute weather updates, ideal for real-time changes in weather conditions.

  • Weather Underground ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.5)

    Best for crowdsourced weather data from personal weather stations, providing highly localized information.

  • Meteoblue ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.6)

    Offers advanced weather forecasting tools, including high-resolution weather maps and models.

  • Windguru ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.4)

    Specializes in wind, wave, and weather forecasts, perfect for outdoor activities like surfing or sailing.

  • YR (Norwegian Meteorological Institute) ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.3)

    Highly accurate global forecasts, with a focus on Europe, using data from the Norwegian Meteorological Institute.

  • NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5.0)

    Best for authoritative weather warnings, climate data, and in-depth scientific analysis of global weather patterns.

  • Met Office (UK) ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5.0)

    The official UK weather service offering detailed and highly reliable weather forecasts and climate information for the UK and globally.

  • Bureau of Meteorology (Australia) ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (4.9)

    Australia's official weather service, best for detailed forecasts, severe weather warnings, and climate observations specific to Australia.

  • Ventusky ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.6)

    Offers beautiful, real-time weather visualization maps showing wind, temperature, precipitation, and other weather variables worldwide.

  • Windfinder ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.4)

    Focuses on wind forecasts for outdoor activities such as sailing, windsurfing, and paragliding, with global coverage.

  • Environment Canada Weather ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (5.0)

    Canada's official weather service providing accurate and detailed forecasts, alerts, and warnings for the entire country.

  • Meteoalarm ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.3)

    Provides pan-European weather warnings and alerts, focusing on severe weather conditions.

Recipes for Camps, Ranches, and Stations

  • Bulk Recipes ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.6)

    This site offers over 500 recipes designed for cooking in large quantities, ideal for camps, ranches, and stations. You can easily scale recipes up or down based on the number of people.

  • Mountain Kitchen ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.5)

    Featuring over 300 recipes for feeding large groups, with a focus on hearty, filling meals that are perfect for outdoor settings like ranches or remote stations.

  • Campfire Kitchen ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.3)

    Specializes in camp-style meals that are simple to prepare in bulk. The site offers over 150 recipes, focusing on open-fire cooking and easy-to-source ingredients.

  • AllRecipes - Bulk Cooking ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (4.9)

    With thousands of recipes, this section of AllRecipes focuses on bulk cooking for events, camps, and large gatherings. You can find both classic and creative meals to suit any crowd.

  • Chuck Wagon Cooking ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.4)

    For those running cattle ranches or outdoor stations, this site provides over 200 traditional cowboy-style recipes, perfect for cooking in bulk over an open fire or on the go.

  • Sydney Ranch Cooks ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.5)

    This Australian-based site offers more than 100 ranch-style recipes, designed for feeding teams at stations and ranches. Many recipes focus on using local and seasonal ingredients.

  • Army Recipes ★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (4.8)

    A site dedicated to military-style bulk cooking, with over 500 recipes tested in large-scale settings. Ideal for preparing nutritious, cost-effective meals for big groups.

  • Camp Chef Bulk Cooking Recipes ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.3)

    Focused on bulk cooking for outdoor adventures, this site provides recipes that cater to groups of 10 or more. Most recipes are simple to prepare using portable camp gear.

  • Homesteader's Supply ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.4)

    This site offers over 200 recipes that cater to large homestead families and ranches, with a focus on using fresh, homegrown ingredients to create meals in bulk.

  • Cooks.com - Bulk Recipes ★ ★ ★ ★ ☆ (4.5)

    With a large database of bulk recipes, this site is perfect for those who need to feed large groups efficiently. Recipes include quantities for 20 or more people.
